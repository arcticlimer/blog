Installing Armbian into an old TV Box

Vinícius Müller on July 24, 2022

Recently I've found a rather old unused TV Box and decided to install Armbian in it to power my homelab (or roomlab, actually).

The box that I have here is a Nexbox A95X. This video explains pretty much well what you should do in order to install Armbian on yours. But I ran into some problems when trying it in my A95X-B7N model:

  • In the video, when the extlinux/extlinux.conf file is being edited, I had to uncomment the meson-gxl-s905x-p212.dtb instead of the meson-gxm-q200.dtb FTD directive.
  • Wi-Fi is not working. From the video comments, it appears that installing other versions than bullseye might make Wi-Fi work.
