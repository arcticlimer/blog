Setting XServer's Background Image

Vinícius Müller on October 30, 2021

If you have NixOS managing your XServer's session you can easily set your desktop background image by moving an image to ~/.background-image. If you want to customize it furthermore, you can check the options available in the services.xserver.desktopManager.wallpaper namespace.
