Recovering from broken bootloader

Vinícius Müller on September 18, 2022

NixOS provides some really nice properties, such as the ability to rollback your config using a menu in the bootloader if you need. But what if there is no bootloader? Recently the power went off when nixos-rebuild switch was rebuilding the bootloader and I got locked out of my PC.

In order to repair that, there is no big secret: you will need just a live NixOS USB. Then just mount your partitions and run from the ISO: nixos-install --flake <path_to_your_config>, then Nix will already notice that all the required packages are already in your nix store and will just fix your bootloader.
